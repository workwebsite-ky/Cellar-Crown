Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

How long does a typical cleaning take?
Duration depends on your home's size and the service type. A standard residential clean for a 3-bedroom home typically takes 2.5–4 hours. Deep cleans may take longer. We'll give you a time estimate when you book.
Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?
No — we bring everything. Our team arrives fully equipped with premium, eco-certified professional-grade cleaning supplies, tools, and equipment. You don't need to provide a thing.
Are your cleaners background-checked?
Absolutely. Every Cellar Crown team member undergoes a thorough background check, identity verification, and reference screening before joining our team. Your safety and trust are our highest priority.
What if I'm not satisfied with the cleaning?
We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're unhappy with any part of our service, contact us within 24 hours and we will return to re-clean the area at absolutely no charge.
Do you offer recurring cleaning plans?
Yes! We offer weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly maintenance plans at discounted rates. Our recurring clients receive priority scheduling and a dedicated team that learns your home's specific needs over time.
How do I book a cleaning?
Simply visit our Contact page, fill out the form, or email us directly at contactglanders@gmail.com. We'll respond within a few hours to confirm your booking and answer any questions.
