Quick Answers

Before You Contact Us

How quickly will you respond to my inquiry?
We guarantee a response within 2 hours during business hours (Mon–Sat, 7AM–7PM). For inquiries submitted outside business hours, you'll hear from us first thing the next morning.
Can I book same-day cleaning?
Same-day availability depends on our schedule. Imperial Crown clients receive same-day priority. For other packages, we typically book 24–48 hours in advance. Contact us directly for urgent requests.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and cash. Payment is processed after the cleaning is complete and you're fully satisfied. No upfront payments required for first-time bookings.
Do I need to be home during the cleaning?
Not at all. Many clients provide a key or entry code. Our team is fully vetted, insured, and trusted. You'll receive a completion notification and photo report when we're done.
